Principles of Design Assignment

Signal-to-Noise Ratio

This is an example of signal-to-noise ratio becasue there is an even amount of relevant content and non relevant content. The simple background evens out the area of content.

Hick's Law

This is an example of Hick's Law becasue each header has subheaders. The user first chooses what category they want and then can go into even more detail with what they want to choose. This decreases decision time and makes decisions simpler.

Rule of Thirds

D2L content is split up into three groups- My Cources, SAFE E-Learning, and News.

Ockham's Razor

The apple website is very effective yet very simple. There is minimal content but it still has a lot of functionality.

Affordance

The D2L login button is intended for function. The button also appears to be 3D.
